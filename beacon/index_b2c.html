How does AdsNearMe help business?

Surely, you have thought about a mobile application for your business and for personal ads – it is convenient for your clients, which means it is profitable for you. “Mobilization” is already becoming a trend, but for designing your own application, you need to withdraw funds from turnover, which is not always possible. So, what is the way out?

How do you attract new customers? Billboard advertising? Handing out flyers? Radio advertisement? Newspaper or website advertisements? The weakness of these channels is too general audience – like using a sledgehammer to crack a nut, as specialists put it. With AdsNearMe you get a pinpoint solution – attract new customers who come to you already interested, although they had no idea about it just a second ago. This is a new level of business.

Intriguing with offers!

Let us assume that a person wants to buy a couch. He opens AdsNearMe app, selects “Furniture” section and sees all promotion offers on his screen. One shop offers couches from a manufacturer with up to 35% discount. Another one is raffling a tour to Cyprus. The third one has free delivery and assemblage. And our client does not choose between goods and services of different companies, but between their offers.

With the help of AdsNearMe you can post and receive advertisements posted in your vicinity! AdsNearMe allows you to call the advertisement number in a single click, send messages conveniently, bookmark selected ads, etc. Now have a look at how AdsNearMe brings you clients…

Geofencing technology

Imagine that a person is going to buy a couch, with money in his wallet. While he is waiting for the traffic light, his phone receives a push message, “Couches with framework from natural wood, a 20% discount today – 300 meters from you.” What is the probability of this person heading for that shop right now? Almost 100%, isn’t it? We type in coordinates of an outlet into an application and circle a range, for instance, 500 meters. And coming within that range, a person who has AdsNearMe application installed gets a push message with your offer – even if his phone is in a waiting mode. Now look at how the “younger brother” of Geofencing technology – Apple iBeacon & Google Eddystone – works.

Geolocation marketing technology iBeacon and Eddystone

Unlike Geofencing, Apple iBeacon and Google Eddystone technologies work with clients within a walking distance from an outlet. The outlet has tracking devices with the working range from 10 cm to 200 meters installed in it. The signal from tracking devices interacts with a phone application, and our potential customer receives a push message with a promotion offer. The probability of reacting to the message is also close to 100%, as this person is passing by the outlet. How do these technologies work together? The answer is on the next slide…
